So you want to buy a home. Here are some quick tips.

Real Estate is always changing for many reasons.

Real estate is never static because money, land and people are constantly moving. Even the condition of a piece of land or the improvements on it, like homes, barns or a mall, are always changing over time. Here are some things to consider when purchasing a home:

(Not in any order of priority)
  • Cash available for the purchase.
  • Personal assets in terms of skills for property maintenance and improvement vs. the need to hire contractors to do jobs.
  • Lifestyle and social preferences.
These are some of the steps for purchasing a home efficiently:
(In order of priority)
  • Interview lenders. Follow their instructions for receiving any gifts from family. Get mortgage preapproval and your price range.
  • Interview reputable real estate agents for representation and sign a buyer representation agreement.
  • Go shopping with a written list of your "must have" and "want to have" features.
